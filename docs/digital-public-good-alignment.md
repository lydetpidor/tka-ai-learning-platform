# Digital Public Good Alignment

## Overview

The TKA AI Learning Platform is being developed as scalable educational infrastructure aligned with Digital Public Goods principles.

---

## Core Alignment Areas

### Open Source
The platform supports open collaboration and transparency.

### Accessibility
Designed for underserved and low-connectivity environments.

### Interoperability
Future systems are designed for government and institutional integration.

### Scalability
Infrastructure supports regional deployment and adaptation.

### Equity
Focus on inclusion for vulnerable children and communities.

### SDG Contribution
Aligned with global sustainable development priorities.

---

## Long-Term Goal

To contribute toward open, resilient, and child-centered digital learning infrastructure for emerging markets.
