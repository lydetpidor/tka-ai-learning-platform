# Climate Resilience Learning Framework

## Purpose

This framework outlines how the TKA AI Learning Platform supports climate resilience and learning continuity for vulnerable children and communities.

---

## Challenges Addressed

- Climate-related school disruption
- Floods and extreme weather
- Unequal access to education
- Low-connectivity environments
- Teacher shortages
- Digital divide

---

## Solution Components

### Adaptive Learning
AI-personalized learning pathways support continuous learning.

### Low-bandwidth Delivery
Designed for underserved communities with limited infrastructure.

### Offline-first Learning
Students can continue learning during disruptions.

### Climate Education
Integrated sustainability and climate adaptation learning.

### Teacher Resilience
Teacher upskilling for digital and blended learning delivery.

---

## Expected Impact

- Increased education continuity
- Improved digital resilience
- Stronger future workforce readiness
- Enhanced climate awareness
- Greater inclusion for vulnerable learners
