# AI Safety and Child Protection Principles

## Commitment

TKA is committed to responsible, ethical, and child-safe AI systems.

---

## Core Principles

### Child-Centered Design
Technology should improve learning, safety, and wellbeing.

### Privacy Protection
We minimize unnecessary personal data collection.

### Ethical AI
AI systems should reduce bias and support inclusion.

### Transparency
Users should understand how AI systems support learning.

### Human Oversight
Teachers and guardians remain essential in learning processes.

### Accessibility
AI systems should support underserved communities.

---

## Data Principles

- Data minimization
- Responsible storage
- Controlled access
- No exploitative monetization
- Child-safe governance

---

## Future Development

- AI governance review systems
- Safety audits
- Ethical learning standards
- Responsible AI deployment frameworks
