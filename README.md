# TKA AI Learning Platform

AI-powered climate resilience and future-skills learning infrastructure for underserved children and schools in emerging markets.

---

## Vision

Empowering 70 million learners and educators across Asia by 2035 through AI-powered resilient education systems.

---

## Problem

Millions of children across emerging markets remain excluded from future-ready education due to:

- Limited digital infrastructure
- Climate-related disruptions
- Lack of AI and digital literacy
- Limited teacher capacity
- Unequal access to quality learning
- Weak continuity systems during crises

Traditional education systems are struggling to prepare children for a future shaped by artificial intelligence, climate change, automation, and digital transformation.

---

## Solution

Tech for Kids Academy (TKA) is building an AI-powered learning ecosystem designed for:

- Personalized AI-assisted learning
- Climate-resilient education delivery
- Low-bandwidth and underserved environments
- Teacher upskilling
- SDG-aligned project-based learning
- Entrepreneurship and problem-solving education

The platform combines:

- Adaptive AI learning
- Teacher dashboards
- Learning analytics
- Climate resilience modules
- Offline-first capabilities
- School and government deployment systems

---

## Current Traction

- 20,000+ students reached
